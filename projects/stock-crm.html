The Vision

StockCRM was born from the need to bridge the gap between inventory management and customer relationship management. Most small to medium businesses in Morocco were using separate, disconnected tools. I built StockCRM to be a unified 'Business OS'.

Architecture

The system consists of four main pillars: a React-based Web Dashboard, a Flutter Mobile App, a secure Admin Portal, and a high-performance Node.js/PostgreSQL microservices backend.
